Admission Process Overview

We welcome applications from families who seek a rigorous, values-based curriculum anchored in computational learning and modern logic frameworks.

Our admissions track is designed to evaluate core baseline developmental skills rather than strict academic testing, ensuring a balanced, supportive integration process.

Required Documentation

  • 📝 Completed Registration Form
  • 👶 Original Child Birth Certificate copy
  • 📸 Two recent passport photographs
  • 📊 Previous school academic report cards (if applicable)
  • 💉 Up-to-date child immunization records
